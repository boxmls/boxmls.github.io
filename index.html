BoxMLS

Our open-source platform imports, standardizes and continuously monitors nationwide MLS data for changes, giving you direct access to the data from a single database that you own.

Platform Features

Real Time Data

Listing updates continuously pushed to your systems instantly. Price changes, status changes, new listings, everything - instantly. Any slower than real time and you're losing out on opportunities and deals.

Compatibility

Work within the existing organized Real Estate model, compatible with all legacy MLS platforms, allowing for easy migration.

Extendability

When not provided by your MLS, we can provide latitude and longitude coordinates for all listings.

Freedom and Ownership

You’re in control - the tech can’t be taken away from you Tech companies you depend on can pivot or get acquired, leaving your business exposed (risk of disruption)

Flexibility

Many products don’t integrate. Confusing software fragmentation between MLS - Broker - Agent platforms

Cloud Technology

Lower your cost of ownership and maintenance.
